What is a sign?

The zodiac sign of a person is defined from the observation of the Sun (solar sign), checking in which constellation it is located at the time of birth of the individual, for example. According to astrology, zodiac signs have the power to define a person's personality or influence the course of a person's life. It is common for people to be interested in knowing what their sign is and the characteristics related to it. The sun sign is the best known and results from the interaction of planet Earth with the Sun. In the zodiac, there are twelve signs in total: Aries, Taurus, Gemini, Cancer, Leo, Virgo, Libra, Scorpio, Sagittarius, Capricorn, Aquarius and Pisces.
